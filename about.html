About Me

My name is Ellen Yu and I'm from Queens, NYC. I graduated with an Advanced Regents Diploma from Stuyvesant High School and am currently a sophomore studying CS, Linguistics, and Energy and the Environment at Duke University.

I'm deeply interested in using tech to address climate change and environmental justice. Currently, I'm working on a solar-powered desk clock and teaching myself some electrical engineering in the process. I'm also self-learning architecture and sustainable design, with the goal of integrating these principles into future projects. Beyond that, I love exploring the intersections of language, culture, and technology. This semester, I'm taking "Computational Approach to Human Language".

I absolutely love getting to know new languages, people, and places. I look to my favorite creators, like Javier Senosiain, Antoni Gaudí, and Claude Monet, for creative inspiration.
