About Me

My name is Ellen Yu and I'm from Queens, NYC. I graduated with an Advanced Regents Diploma from Stuyvesant High School and am currently a freshman studying CS+Linguistics at Duke University.

I am an interdisciplinary creator blending technology, language, and art to build interactive systems and digital experiences that are grounded in both the visual and the concrete. I look to natural forms for guidance — asymmetry, repetition, and the beauty of imperfection, just as my favorite artists, Gaudí and Monet, did.

I absolutely love getting to know new languages, people, and places.
